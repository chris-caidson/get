The Step-by-Step Launch Process

Launching a new Associate is a step by step process that consists of 2 to 3 Launch Calls.

Follow the steps below and use the language when applicable. Keep a record of where you are with your Associate using the New Customer Checklist.

  1. Have your new Customer visit Isagenix.com and log in to their back office.

  2. Show them the purple “Click Here to Become an Associate” button.

    • For Customers not ready to share: “When people start asking you about it and you would like to earn cash for their referrals, you’re going to click here. It takes 5 minutes and it’s very simple.”
    • For those ready to share: “Knowing that you’re interested in getting the company to cover the cost of your products, go ahead and click the button on the left side of your screen that says “Click Here to Become an Associate.” It’s a simple process that requires you to watch a 3 minute video. I’ll just stay on mute while you watch it and let me know when you’re done.”

  3. Show them their AutoShip.

    • Have them put their AutoShip date on their calendar and put a reminder in their phone to contact you 3 days before that date to see where they are in their goals and make a recommendation of how to go forward.
  4. Show them the Place Order button in case they need a partial order during the month.

  5. NEW ASSOCIATES ONLY** Show them their Placement Tree

    • “Go to Team at the top and click View Placement Tree. Go to the bottom where it looks like a family tree. This is your team. When you get somebody started this is where they’re going to go. Have you noticed you have (2) people already on your team?”
    • They will say “How did this happen?”

    • “That is the beauty of the compensation plan. Have you seen the compensation plan video?”

      • If no: “On our GlobalExecTeam.com website there is a Get Paid To Share tab. I’ll send you a direct link to it so you have it in your inbox. You definitely want to check that out after our call.”
      • If yes: “Do you remember what you saw in the video? Well, those (2) people are pat of your team and you will be paid on their orders forever.”
  6. Show them www.globalexecteam.com.
  7. Help them make their list – The Home Plate Tab (typically Launch Call #2)
  8. Help them refine their list – (Launch Call #3 – or #2 if they did the FB Post and List in Call #1)
  9. Inoculate Your Associate
