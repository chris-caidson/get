Step 5

Get Started - Step 5: Get Them Started Right

1. Collect Their Information

Don’t know what comes in each pack or what to ask for? Don’t worry! We have you covered with our easy Order Form.

2. Open Their Account

Step-by-step instructions to ensure your friends get everything they need to succeed.

3. Connect Them to Our Team

Add and welcome them to the Facebook team groups (Global Exec Team, Get Fit, and Get Free) and do a Welcome Call with another team member.

4. Send Their Welcome Text

This text includes their user name, password and autoship date. It also directs them to our Product Resource Page to review videos and print trackers to review as they await their system’s arrival.

5. Set Up a 20-Minute Launch Call

During this call you’ll show them their Back Office, the IsaBody Challenge, the Contagious NRG Training site and answer any questions they have about their system.

The Launch Call Template can be split over one to three calls depending on the customer. Our goal is to make sure they are given all the information they need to manage their account and enroll their friends and accountability partners.

6. Check on Their Progress

Check in daily by text or phone for the first 14 days. Ask how their first shake was and when they plan to do their first cleanse. Check in on their cleanse day to cheer them on. Check in 5 days before their autoship to help them adjust their next order. Our goal is to help them feel supported and get their best results.

After their first two day cleanse use the Day 5 Follow-Up Script to encourage them to Text A Few friends and get their next box paid for.
